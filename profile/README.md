# 🚀 About GrowMinion

GrowMinion LLC is a **Digital Marketing, AI Automation, and Software Development Company** dedicated to helping local businesses generate predictable revenue through technology and data-driven marketing.

Rather than offering disconnected marketing services, we build complete **Revenue Engines** that combine SEO, paid advertising, AI automation, high-converting websites, and custom SaaS products to help businesses attract, convert, and retain more customers.

Today, GrowMinion proudly supports **100+ companies** across North America with scalable digital growth solutions.
